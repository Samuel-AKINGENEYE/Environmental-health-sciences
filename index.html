About Our Program

Environmental Health Sciences

Our program provides students with the knowledge and skills to assess, control, and prevent environmental factors that can potentially affect human health. Through a combination of classroom instruction, laboratory work, and field experiences, students gain practical expertise in this critical public health discipline.

The curriculum builds on foundational concepts to explore advanced topics in environmental health, preparing students for careers in government agencies, private industry, non-profit organizations, and academic research.
